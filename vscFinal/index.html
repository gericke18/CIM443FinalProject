Born in Boston

Made in Miami

My name is Nolan Gericke. I’m proficient in web and graphic design and am currently majoring in Interactive Media at the University of Miami.

I first became interested in digital media upon returning from a high school scuba diving trip in Hawaii. I had amassed a large collection of photos of sharks, sea turtles, dolphins, and other various wildlife, but I wasn’t satisifed with the way many of the photos came out with many of them tarnished by backscatter, lighting issues and dull colors.

It was in this moment that I opened photoshop for the first time. I wanted to make these pictures beautiful, and I became impressed with how a few simple tools could recapture the beauty and awe that I’d felt when I first took them. I like making uninspiring things beautiful, and continue to refine my skills to become an established digital media artist and graphic designer.
